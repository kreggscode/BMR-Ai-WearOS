Privacy Policy

BMI AI-Powered - Wear OS BMI Calculator

Effective Date: November 10, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how BMI AI-Powered ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Wear OS application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location data.

Health Data

The app calculates BMI based on height and weight values you input. This data:

  • Is processed locally on your device
  • Is not stored or transmitted
  • Is used only for BMI calculation and display
  • Is cleared when you close the app

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, including:

  • App usage frequency
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports (if applicable)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate and display BMI results
  • Provide AI-powered health analysis (when internet available)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure app stability and performance

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit any personal or health data to third parties.

AI Analysis Service

When you request AI analysis:

  • Only BMI value and category are sent to Pollinations.AI
  • No personal identifiers are included
  • Data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
  • Responses are cached temporarily for offline use

Third-Party Services

The app uses:

  • Pollinations.AI: For generating health analysis text (optional feature)
  • Google Play Services: For app updates and crash reporting (if enabled)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • Network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • App follows Android security best practices

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
